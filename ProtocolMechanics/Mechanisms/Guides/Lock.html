Lock

Lock your PENDLE for vePENDLE. You can also extend the duration of locked PENDLE.

  1. Go to Lock

  1. Enter the desired amount of PENDLE to lock

  1. Select the locking duration

  1. Verify the amount of vePENDLE to be received and the corresponding APY

  1. Approve the transaction and Lock!
