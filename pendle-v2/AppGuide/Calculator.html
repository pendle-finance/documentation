Calculator

The Pendle Yield Calculator helps you to predict the potential returns from various strategies on Pendle, letting you identify the best yield opportunities available.

Tutorialâ€‹

  1. Go to Pendle App and click on "Calculator".

  1. Select one or more of the 3 strategies.

  1. Pick an Underlying Asset and the corresponding Maturity Date from the dropdowns.

  1. Select the amount of Initial Capital you wish to supply. You can toggle the input between USD and Underlying by clicking on the respective buttons in the top right corner.

  1. Key in your expected Average Future APY. This can also be used to test out the profit scenarios for various APYs. Note: Default set to the current Underlying APY.

  1. If LP is selected, there will be an additional field to vary the Target Lock Duration and Target PENDLE Locked. You can also use this to check the precise minimum requirements for Max Boost.
